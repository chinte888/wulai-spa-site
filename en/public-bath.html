Public Bath

Public Bath

Built along the mountain and river, the male and female public baths preserve natural beauty.
The bath is divided into four pools: high, medium, low temp, and cold spring to promote circulation.
The ladies' bath features outdoor pools and lounge chairs to enjoy a lazy sunbath under the trees.

Hot Spring Pool

Pools & Facilities

Both male and female baths are equipped with back-washing water jets and bubble massage beds to enjoy hydrotherapy. There are also Finnish saunas and steam rooms for different spa experiences.

Hydrotherapy

Hydrotherapy Massage

Back water jets and bubble beds stimulate muscles with water pressure and bubbles, relieving tension, which is the most enjoyable extension after a hot spring bath.

Massage Service

Body Massage & Relax

Professional massage services including full-body acupressure, aromatic oil massage, body exfoliation, cupping, facial care, etc., to relax your body and mind completely.

Sauna

Sauna & Steam

Finnish wood sauna. Sweating in high temperature and then entering the cold pool, alternating hot and cold, is one of the most popular experiences in the public bath area.
