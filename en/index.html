Wulai Hot Spring · Wulai Spa Resort

Ascending along Nanshi Creek, mountains layer and mist lingers year-round.
The hot spring water here is colorless and odorless. The weak acid carbonated hydrogen sodium spring stays warm,
a hot spring land passed down through generations by the Atayal tribe, and a corner outside the city
for you to cast off all worries.

Public Bath

Public Bath Pools

Built along the mountain and river, the separate male and female public baths feature four pools of high, medium, low temperature and cold spring. Equipped with back-washing water jets, bubble massage beds, and sauna, it is the simplest way to unwind.

Private Room

Premium Private Rooms

The outdoor open-air private rooms are built with natural stones, allowing you to enjoy the mountain scenery while bathing. The indoor family private rooms are equipped with hot and cold double pools, perfect for private family moments.
