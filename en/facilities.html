Facilities

Beyond hot spring baths, the resort prepares suitable corners for every moment—
whether it is a business meeting, a chat after dining, or simply sitting down,
listening quietly to the sound of Nanshi Creek.

Conference Room · Capacity 30–50 People

Conference Room

Equipped with large whiteboard, projection equipment, and spacious conference tables and chairs, suitable for annual plans, business meetings, etc. You can also visit nearby scenic spots and Wulai Old Street.

Cafe

Elegant Cafe

Located on the first floor, it offers a peaceful rest area after bathing, serving various hot and cold drinks and snacks. Feel free to ask service staff for options.

Outdoor Lounge

Outdoor Lounge Seats

Located on the pedestrian path outside the resort, next to the mountains and river. You can rest while enjoying the view, watching white egrets fly over the stream, and listening to the stream flow.
